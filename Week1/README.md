# Week 1 - Activity 1: Descriptive Analysis using Data Aggregation

## Objective
This activity performs an initial descriptive analysis of the attached dataset using data aggregation techniques only.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Excel dataset (.xlsx)

## Steps Performed
1. Loaded the dataset from Excel
2. Grouped the data by category
3. Applied aggregation functions:
   - Sum of sales
   - Mean of sales
   - Count of sales records
   - Sum of quantity
   - Mean of quantity
4. Generated a simple chart to visualize total sales by category

## Aggregated Result

| Category | Sales Sum | Sales Mean | Sales Count | Quantity Sum | Quantity Mean |
|----------|-----------|------------|-------------|--------------|---------------|
| A | 18010 | 545.76 | 33 | 282 | 8.55 |
| B | 22154 | 615.39 | 36 | 343 | 9.53 |
| C | 18213 | 587.52 | 31 | 273 | 8.81 |

## Story Behind the Data
The aggregated result shows that Category B is the strongest performer overall. 
It has the highest total sales, highest average sales, highest number of records, and highest total quantity sold. 
Category A is the weakest performer. Category C performs better than A in average sales and average quantity, but it is still below Category B overall.
